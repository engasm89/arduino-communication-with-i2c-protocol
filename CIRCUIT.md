## Connections

- I2C bus: `SDA`, `SCL` with pull-up resistors to VCC, common `GND`.
- Device address e.g., `0x3C`.

## Diagram (ASCII)

```
 SDA <----> Device SDA (pull-up)
 SCL <----> Device SCL (pull-up)
 GND ----- Common ground
```
